§02 Coastal & Patrol Forces Pasukan Penjaga Pantai & Patroli

Optimized for domestic EEZ boundaries, chokepoints, and littoral operations. They focus on sovereignty enforcement and rapid strike tactics rather than deep-ocean battles. Dioptimalkan untuk operasi batas teritorial ZEE domestik, selat, dan wilayah litoral. Fokus pada penegakan kedaulatan maritim dan serangan cepat.

Offshore Patrol Vessel

ConstabularyKeamanan

The maritime police officer. A constabulary ship optimized for seakeeping in rough weather, patrolling vast Exclusive Economic Zones (EEZ) to counter illegal fishing, piracy, and smuggling. Sang aparat penegak hukum maritim. Kapal keamanan maritim yang dioptimalkan untuk ketahanan operasional tinggi, berpatroli di ZEE untuk menekan penangkapan ikan ilegal, bajak laut, dan penyelundupan.

SizeTonase 1,500 - 3,000 tons
RangeJarak Jangkau 6,000 - 10,000+ nm
EnduranceDaya Tahan 21 - 35+ days
ArmamentSenjata 30mm-76mm Gun

Corvette

Littoral CombatTempur Litoral

The light, fast fighter. The smallest 'true' warship, optimized for coastal combat. Punches well above its weight, carrying anti-ship missiles, compact air defenses, and anti-submarine torpedoes. Sang petarung ringan yang gesit. Kapal kombatan terkecil yang sesungguhnya, dioptimalkan untuk pertempuran pesisir. Dilengkapi rudal anti-kapal berat dan pertahanan udara taktis.

SizeTonase 1,000 - 3,500 tons
RangeJarak Jangkau 2,000 - 4,000 nm
EnduranceDaya Tahan 10 - 21 days
ArmamentSenjata AShMs, SAMs, 76mm Gun

Fast Attack Craft

Rapid StrikeSerang Cepat

The coastal mosquito. Sacrifices endurance and armor for blistering speeds and low radar cross-sections. Armed heavily with heavy anti-ship missiles for hit-and-run swamp tactics in chokepoints. Sang nyamuk pemburu. Mengorbankan ketahanan laut dan pelindung baja demi kecepatan luar biasa dan profil radar rendah. Sangat mematikan di selat sempit untuk serangan dadakan.

SizeTonase 50 - 800 tons
RangeJarak Jangkau 800 - 1,500 nm
EnduranceDaya Tahan 3 - 7 days
ArmamentSenjata Heavy Anti-Ship Missiles

§03 Blue Water Fleet (Ocean-Going) Armada Samudra (Blue Water Fleet)

These major surface combatants are engineered for high-intensity, global fleet encounters. They serve as multi-role escorts and high-end air shields for task forces. Kapal-kapal kombatan utama permukaan ini dirancang untuk pertempuran samudra terbuka intensitas tinggi. Berfungsi sebagai pengawal multi-peran dan perisai udara armada.

Frigate

Multi-Role EscortPengawal Multi-Peran

The versatile fleet workhorse. Possesses a highly balanced suite of sensors and weaponry to independently hunt submarines (ASW), defend against missile strikes (AAW), and protect larger task forces. Sang kuda beban armada serbaguna. Kombatan dengan sistem sensor dan senjata yang sangat seimbang untuk memburu kapal selam (ASW), pertahanan udara (AAW), dan pengawalan kapal induk.

SizeTonase 3,500 - 8,000+ tons
RangeJarak Jangkau 4,000 - 8,000+ nm
EnduranceDaya Tahan 21 - 30+ days
ArmamentSenjata VLS, Torpedoes, AShMs

Destroyer

Advanced Air ShieldPerisai Udara Maju

The fleet's shield. Heavily armed surface combatant specialized in Anti-Air Warfare (AAW). Features large-capacity Vertical Launch Systems (VLS) and advanced phased-array radars (like AEGIS). Sang perisai pelindung armada. Kombatan permukaan berat dengan spesialisasi pertahanan udara (AAW) area. Dilengkapi puluhan sel VLS dan sensor radar modern jangkauan jauh.

SizeTonase 7,000 - 10,000+ tons
RangeJarak Jangkau 4,500 - 8,000+ nm
EnduranceDaya Tahan 21 - 30+ days
ArmamentSenjata Large VLS, SAMs, Gun

Cruiser

Fleet CommandKomando Armada

The heavy-hitter & command ship. The largest non-carrier surface combatants. Serves as flagship command centers, deploying massive VLS arrays for long-range offensive area dominance. Sang pemukul berat & kapal komando. Kapal kombatan non-induk terbesar. Berfungsi sebagai pusat komando laksamana, membawa ratusan sel rudal untuk mendominasi area samudra.

SizeTonase 10,000 - 15,000+ tons
RangeJarak Jangkau 6,000 - 10,000+ nm
EnduranceDaya Tahan 30+ days
ArmamentSenjata 120+ VLS Cells, Command Sys

§04 Strategic & Support Assets Aset Strategis & Logistik Pendukung

These massive capital hulls project power globally, enforce undersea nuclear deterrence, or supply essential logistics to sustain task groups on remote station. Lambung kapal raksasa ini memproyeksikan kekuatan udara secara global, membawa penangkal nuklir bawah air, atau menyuplai logistik penting di laut lepas.

Aircraft Carrier

Power ProjectionProyeksi Kekuatan

The floating airbase. Armed with an air wing of 60-90+ fighters, early warning, and electronic warfare planes. Can establish air superiority thousands of miles from domestic borders. Sang pangkalan udara terapung. Senjata utamanya adalah skuadron udara beranggotakan 60-90+ jet tempur dan pesawat intai. Menjadi perwakilan kedaulatan negara secara global.

SizeTonase 60,000 - 100K+ tons
RangeJarak Jangkau Unlimited (Nuclear)
EnduranceDaya Tahan 20+ Years (Core life)
ArmamentSenjata 60-90+ Combat Aircraft

Amphibious Assault Ship

ExpeditionaryEkspedisi Militer

The mobile invasion platform. Houses and deploys marines, helicopters, landing craft, and vertical-takeoff jets from internal floodable dock chambers and flat-top runways. Sang pangkalan invasi bergerak. Mengangkut ribuan pasukan infanteri laut (marinir) beserta helikopter, kapal pendarat, dan jet tempur lepas landas vertikal (VSTOL).

SizeTonase 20,000 - 45,000 tons
RangeJarak Jangkau 7,000 - 9,500+ nm
EnduranceDaya Tahan 30 - 45+ days
ArmamentSenjata Helos, LCAC, Marines

Submarine (SSN/SSK)

Stealth HunterPemburu Siluman

The silent hunter. Designed for sub-surface dominance, hunting enemy ships, or hiding off-shore to provide strategic nuclear deterrence via ballistic silos (SSBN). Sang pemburu senyap. Beroperasi tanpa terdeteksi di kedalaman samudra untuk menghancurkan kapal musuh, atau membawa rudal balistik nuklir strategis (SSBN).

SizeTonase 1,000 - 20,000+ tons
RangeJarak Jangkau Unlimited (Nuclear)
EnduranceDaya Tahan 90+ days (Food limit)
ArmamentSenjata Torpedoes, Cruise Missiles

Support & Auxiliary

Fleet LogisticsLogistik Armada

The fleet's lifeline. Logistics hulls (replenishment tankers, ammunition carriers) that fuel, supply, and maintain combat ships on station, extending operations from days to months. Sang tali nyawa armada. Kapal logistik pendukung (tanker BBM maritim, amunisi, suku cadang) yang memastikan armada kombatan dapat terus beroperasi tanpa harus pulang ke pangkalan.

SizeTonase 2,000 - 50,000+ tons
RangeJarak Jangkau 10,000+ nm
EnduranceDaya Tahan 30 - 45+ days
ArmamentSenjata Defensive Only (CIWS)

Battleship (Historical)

Historical KingsLegenda Sejarah

The armored dreadnought. Heavy metal legacy. Defined by thick steel girders and giant 16-inch guns. Obsoleted by the rise of carrier-based aviation that could strike far beyond their guns' reach. Sang raksasa dreadnought lapis baja (Sudah pensiun). Ditandai baja super tebal dan meriam raksasa 16-inci. Menjadi usang seiring bangkitnya penerbangan tempur dari kapal induk.

SizeTonase 30,000 - 70,000+ tons
RangeJarak Jangkau 8,000 - 15,000 nm
EnduranceDaya Tahan ~30 days
ArmamentSenjata Giant 14-18 inch Guns
