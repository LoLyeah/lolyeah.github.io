The Blue Water Fleet

Frigate

üåä Multi-Domain Blue Water Warfare

The **versatile fleet escort**. A balanced warship designed for independent operations or to protect larger ships against air, surface, and sub-surface threats far from home.

Key Capability: üõ°Ô∏è **Balanced Sensors & Versatility**
  • **Armament:** 76-127mm cannon, VLS for SAMs, AShMs, advanced sonar & torpedoes, helicopter(s)
  • **Typical Size:** 3,500 - 8,000+ tons
  • **Cost & Crew:** High; workhorse of modern navies
  • **Role:** ASW, AAW, escort, independent patrol, task group command

Destroyer

üí• Advanced Air & Missile Defense

The **primary air defense shield**. Larger and more capable than a frigate, often specialized in advanced Anti-Air Warfare (AAW) with powerful radar and numerous missile cells.

Key Capability: üì° **Advanced AAW & Command Capacity**
  • **Armament:** 127mm cannon, **Extensive VLS (hundreds of missiles)** for SAMs (long-range), AShMs, land-attack missiles, torpedoes, 2 helicopters
  • **Typical Size:** 7,000 - 10,000+ tons
  • **Cost & Crew:** Very High; critical for fleet air defense
  • **Role:** Fleet escort (especially AAW), ballistic missile defense, strike operations

Cruiser

üí™ Fleet Command & Area Dominance

The **heavy-hitter & fleet commander**. Historically a powerful, independent warship, modern cruisers are typically very large destroyers, serving as command ships with maximum firepower and sensor suites.

Key Capability: üëë **Command & Overwhelming Firepower**
  • **Armament:** Similar to destroyers but *more* of everything. Often dual 127mm guns, vast VLS capacity, advanced sensors.
  • **Typical Size:** 10,000 - 15,000+ tons (modern)
  • **Cost & Crew:** Extremely High; few navies operate them today
  • **Role:** Flagship, major AAW/ASW, surface combat, power projection

‚úàÔ∏è Global Power Projection & Air Superiority

Key Capability: üåç **Project Air Power Globally**

üï∞Ô∏è Historical Naval Dominance

The **king of dreadnoughts**. While no longer in active service, battleships were once the most powerful surface combatants, defined by their enormous guns and heavy armor. Pure surface combat.

Key Capability: üí£ **Massive Guns & Impenetrable Armor**

  • **Cost & Crew:** Very High (in their era); slow, expensive to operate
  • **Role:** Fleet-on-fleet surface combat, shore bombardment
