Key Clinical Directives

DO NOT Routinely Use Antibiotics

Most cases are viral or toxin-mediated. Antibiotics are useless and can worsen outcomes in certain bacterial infections (e.g., E. coli O157:H7) by increasing toxin release. Reserve for suspected Shigella, Listeria, or septic patients.

DO NOT Use Antidiarrheals for Bloody Diarrhea

Inhibiting gut motility can increase toxin absorption time and worsen the illness, potentially leading to complications like toxic megacolon or HUS. Use with extreme caution in non-bloody diarrhea.

Algorithm: RED ALERT — Suspected Botulism

ANY PATIENT PRESENTS WITH NEUROLOGICAL SYMPTOMS

Diplopia · Ptosis · Dysphagia · Dysarthria · Descending Paralysis

▾

DECLARE LEVEL 1 EMERGENCY

This is a medical and public health crisis. Activate hospital command center.

▾

IMMEDIATE CLINICAL ACTIONS

  • ADMIT ALL TO ICU
  • Protect AIRWAY: Frequent respiratory checks. Low threshold for intubation.
  • SUPPORTIVE CARE: Ventilation, nutrition.

IMMEDIATE PHARMACOLOGICAL & PUBLIC HEALTH ACTIONS

  • REQUEST ANTITOXIN NOW
  • DO NOT wait for lab confirmation.
  • Contact State Health Dept / CDC immediately.
  • Identify and recall ALL exposed individuals.

IDENTIFY TOXIDROME

Match clinical picture to likely toxin.

▾
MUSHROOM (AMATOXIN)
▾

SYMPTOMS

Severe V/D 6-24h post-ingestion, followed by a 'honeymoon' phase, then acute liver & renal failure.

▾

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS

  • Aggressive fluid resuscitation
  • Contact Poison Control / Toxicology
  • Activated charcoal if within 1-2h
  • Monitor LFTs, renal function, glucose
  • Silibinin / N-acetylcysteine per protocol
  • Prepare for liver transplant consult
PARALYTIC SHELLFISH POISONING (PSP)
▾

SYMPTOMS

Rapid onset (30-60 min). Perioral numbness/tingling, ataxia, cranial nerve dysfunction, ascending paralysis, respiratory failure.

▾

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS

  • AIRWAY PROTECTION IS PRIORITY
  • Low threshold for intubation
  • Intensive supportive care in ICU
  • No specific antitoxin exists
  • Activated charcoal may be considered
  • Recovery usually within days with support
